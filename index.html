Questions we actually
get asked

Does Recoda upload my recordings anywhere?
No. Recoda runs entirely in your browser. When you finish recording, the file is generated locally and saved to your Downloads folder or local disk. We never see your screen.
What browsers does Recoda support?
Recoda works on all modern browsers that support the MediaStream Recording API, including Chrome, Edge, Brave, and Firefox. Safari support is limited.
Can I record system audio without a mic?
Yes! When choosing your screen source, check the "Share system audio" box. You can capture system sound pure, or mix it with your microphone.
What format are recordings saved in?
By default, recordings are saved as WebM or MP4 (depending on browser support). These are highly optimized formats perfect for sharing.
Is there a recording length limit?
On the Free plan, recordings are capped at 30 minutes. Pro users can record for as long as their disk space allows.
How is this different from Loom or OBS?
Loom forces you to the cloud. OBS is powerful but complex to set up. Recoda sits in the middle: instant start like Loom, local privacy like OBS, with zero setup.
